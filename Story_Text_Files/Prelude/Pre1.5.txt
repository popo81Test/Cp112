: According to legend, in the past, there was a silk goddess who weaved silk from stars to shine a light to guide the people in this village.
: Making the castle prosperous and the crops flourishing. But then one day,
: this goddess changed into an evil spirit, harming the townspeople until the castle was covered in blood and flowed out of the castle.
: Finally, a shaman sent from the city of Sema sealed the goddess in the pond of the castle called Nong Sim and built a mound with 16 Sema leaves stuck in it.
: Which were in the shape of lotus petals, to imprison the spirit of this evil spirit.
: It has been taken care of until now by the shaman's children, preventing the seal from being broken and releasing a curse on the city.
